Latitude and Cloudiness

While there is no clear correlation between latitude and cloudiness, the clearest skies seem to be located between 0-40 degrees.

Figure

Cloudiness is highly variable

Summary

Cloudiness is a fairly inconsistent metric because the same city can experience a variable amount of cloudiness across a week. That said, there are still some observable trends in our results.

A high concentration of cities along the latitudes of 0 and 40 degrees experience clearer skies than cities in the Northern Hemisphere.

Cities in the lower latitudes experience clear skies more often than cloudy skies but there are not enough data points to conclude that there is a significant trend going on between location and cloudiness.
